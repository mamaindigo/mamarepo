BTC Yield Fund

The INDIGO BTC Yield Fund is designed for institutional investors and HNWI looking to optimise their yield earning strategy on BTC holdings.

INDIGO is targeting an APY between 5% and 8%, using the following investment strategy:

The investment objective is pursued by a blend of Automated Market Making (AMM) strategies and BTC denominated loans and other debt instruments structured and originated by our trading team.

INDIGO BTC Yield Fund is committed to always keep its holdings in BTC. Therefore, we will not invest in other coins or engage in non-BTC denominated loans/activities. Rewards generated from BTC operations (MM Fees, Loan principal + Interests) will systematically be re-invested in the fund allowing INDIGO to generate additional yield through compounding.

The goal is to continuously increase your BTC stake with INDIGO whilst you continue holding the asset.
