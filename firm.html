Empowering the Future of
Crypto Investments

At INDIGO Fund, we are investors, innovators, and builders. With roots in Traditional Finance (TradFi), our team has decades of experience navigating global markets, risk management, and capital allocation. But when we saw the transformative potential of crypto and decentralized finance (DeFi), we knew this was where the future was being built. The opportunity was too big to ignore—so we went all in.

INDIGO Fund is more than a long-only fund—we are a multi-strategy investment firm that leverages deep market expertise and a data-driven approach to generate superior risk-adjusted returns.

Our Strategy

We employ a discretionary investment approach, carefully selecting assets and strategies that align with our core philosophy:

Liquid Token Investments

Focused on high-potential digital assets with strong fundamentals and liquidity.

DeFi Yield Generation

Optimizing returns through staking, liquidity pools, and innovative yield farming strategies

Arbitrage Trading

Profound knowledge of the technology & where value is captured in tokens models due to long history of (published) research.

By integrating these strategies, we provide our investors with
compelling opportunities in Web3 and decentralized finance.

Our Vision

At INDIGO Fund, we come from institutional finance, where we built our expertise in risk management, capital allocation, and market analysis. But as digital assets and blockchain technology evolved, it became clear that the opportunities in this space far exceeded anything traditional markets could offer.

Our goal is to bridge institutional investment expertise with the speed, transparency, and innovation of crypto, unlocking new opportunities in decentralized finance and beyond.

We believe in:
Unlocking New Investment Frontiers

Identifying and capturing value in liquid tokens, yield-generating strategies, and arbitrage opportunities.

Empowering a Decentralized Economy

Supporting the growth of Web3, DeFi, and emerging blockchain technologies.

Delivering Sustainable, Risk-Managed Returns

Applying institutional-grade investment strategies to maximize returns while minimizing risk.

We are not just investors—we are builders, researchers, and active participants in the next evolution of global finance. INDIGO Fund is where institutional expertise meets the limitless potential of digital assets
